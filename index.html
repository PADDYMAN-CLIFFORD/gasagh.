Our Aims & Objectives

1

Entrepreneurial Motivation

To motivate students to flourish entrepreneurially by equipping them with skills, knowledge and confidence required to operate a business.

2

Entrepreneurial Culture

To develop an entrepreneurial culture among students for self-reliance.

3

Mentorship & Training

Organize mentorship, workshops and training to gain leadership and business management expertise.

4

Learning from Entrepreneurs

To hear from inspiring entrepreneurs, learn their tips for success, and network with like-minded people.

5

Idea Exchange & Competitions

To exchange business ideas among students, creating a forum for business plans competition and debate and accessibility to business advice and development.

6

Innovative Implementation

To engage in the creative and innovative discipline through implementing business concepts with an entrepreneurial mindset.
