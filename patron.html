DR.MAXWELL ACHEAMPONG

LECTURER, centre for African Studies UEW

Surround yourself with six fools, and you become the seventh fool. Surround yourself with six wise people, and you become the seventh wise person.

Dr. Maxwell Acheampong is celebrated not only as a lecturer but as a mentor, visionary, and steadfast pillar of support for his students. His philosophy of education transcends traditional teaching, emphasizing empowerment, innovation, and personal growth.

Through his guidance, countless students have discovered the confidence to dream bigger, act bolder, and pursue excellence with purpose.

Under his patronage, the Generational Achievers Students Association has thrived creating unparalleled opportunities for students to connect with industry leaders, engage in mentorship programs, and gain real-world entrepreneurial experience.
